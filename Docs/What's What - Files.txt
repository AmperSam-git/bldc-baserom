-------------
Baserom Files
-------------

BLDC2.bps - the baserom itself is contained in this patch
BLDC2.dsc - custom block descriptions as seen in Lunar Magic
BLDC2.m16 - internal regular sprite tiles for Lunar Magic
BLDC2.mwt - internal list of sprites for Lunar Magic
BLDC2.msc - the list of inserted Music as read by Lunar Magic
BLDC2.s16 - internal custom sprite tiles for Lunar Magic
BLDC2.ssc - descriptions and metadata for custom sprite seen in Lunar Magic

@initialize_baserom.bat - initializes the baserom by downloading all the tools
